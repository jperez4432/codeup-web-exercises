What hours is the park open?
This National park is open from the hours of 7am to 10pm, although you can make reservations to stay overnight
Can I build a campfire?
You are allowed to create a campfire, as long as there is no county burn ban in place at the time
Can I drink alcohol in at the park?
It is against park rules to drink or display an alcoholic beverage in a public place at any time
Where can I find information on hiking?
You can find information regarding trails and tips within our brochures at the help desk upon entering!
May i carry my handgun?
Our rules state that you may not show/display or fire your gun in a state park. If you have a valid Handgun License, you may carry your handgun in many state parks. But even with a license, handguns are not allowed in parks that are leased from the federal government.
Can I bring my pet?
You can bring your pets to almost all state parks! However, we do have some rules:
  • Pets must be on a leash at all times
  • You must be with your pet at all times and they cannot be left unattended
  • Pets must have current rabies vaccination and you must have proof with you
How long may i stay at the park?
Generally, you can stay for 14 consecutive days
Is wireless internet access available at the park?
Some areas have WI-FI available. If you search the map it will show these areas
Can i ride my bike at the park?
Almost all state parks offer road or mountain biking, or both! Many parks have long stretches of road (some winding and hilly). Many of our parks have multiuse trails. Visit the map for the best trails!
How much does it cost to enter the park?
The park cost $10 per car upon entrance.
